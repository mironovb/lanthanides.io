Rare Earth Prices & Strategic Metal Pricing

Current prices per kilogram for rare earth elements, strategic metals & semiconductor materials — retail reference and bulk benchmark pricing with full provenance

{{ content }}

lanthanides.io tracks real-world prices for rare earth elements, strategic metals, and semiconductor materials. Each price is sourced from verified, in-stock listings across retail suppliers, industrial wholesalers, and commodity benchmarks — then normalised to USD per kilogram for direct comparison.

We cover {{ site.elements | size }} elements across four categories: light rare earths (lanthanum, cerium, neodymium, praseodymium), heavy rare earths (dysprosium, terbium, gadolinium, yttrium, europium, erbium), strategic metals (tungsten, cobalt, bismuth, tellurium, niobium, tantalum, vanadium, zirconium), and semiconductor metals (gallium, germanium, indium, selenium). Prices are tracked at both retail and bulk tiers, with {{ site.data.price_records | size }} price records currently in the database.
