<div class="element-section info-block">
  <h2 class="section-title">Important Notes</h2>
  <div class="notes-block">
    <div class="note-item">
      <strong>Conflict-mineral compliance</strong>
      Tantalum is the &ldquo;Ta&rdquo; in 3TG. SEC reporting issuers and EU importers must perform supply-chain due diligence under Dodd&ndash;Frank 1502 and the EU Conflict Minerals Regulation. Coltan from artisanal mines in eastern DRC dominates the responsible-sourcing conversation.
    </div>
    <div class="note-item">
      <strong>MLCC substitution capping demand</strong>
      Multilayer ceramic capacitors (Murata, TDK, Samsung Electro-Mechanics) have eaten into Ta capacitor demand at the high end of consumer electronics over the past decade. Ta caps remain dominant where high reliability and stable capacitance over temperature are required.
    </div>
    <div class="note-item">
      <strong>Recycling matters</strong>
      Capacitor scrap and IC sputtering target offcuts are major secondary Ta sources. AMG, GAM, and H.C. Starck (now Taniobis) all run Ta recycling lines that meaningfully offset primary mine supply.
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
