<div class="element-section info-block">
  <h2 class="section-title">Important Notes</h2>
  <div class="notes-block">
    <div class="note-item">
      <strong>Not on the MOFCOM control list</strong>
      Lanthanum is one of the light rare earths excluded from the No.&thinsp;18/2025 medium/heavy REE control basket. La trades freely under standard customs procedures, though shipments containing mixed-RE concentrates may still encounter classification scrutiny.
    </div>
    <div class="note-item">
      <strong>FCC catalyst demand floor</strong>
      Refinery FCC unit demand for La-zeolite catalysts establishes a global price floor independent of magnet/EV demand swings. La supply &mdash; like Ce &mdash; is structurally co-produced with Nd/Pr, so chronic oversupply persists when magnet demand drives the basket.
    </div>
    <div class="note-item">
      <strong>NiMH legacy decline</strong>
      Hybrid-electric NiMH battery demand for mischmetal is structurally declining as Toyota and others migrate hybrid platforms to lithium-ion chemistries. Offsetting growth is muted.
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
