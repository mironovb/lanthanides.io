<div class="element-section info-block">
  <h2 class="section-title">Important Notes</h2>
  <div class="notes-block">
    <div class="note-item">
      <strong>Not on the MOFCOM control list</strong>
      Pr is excluded from the No.&thinsp;18/2025 medium/heavy REE control basket. NdPr oxide trades freely under standard customs procedures.
    </div>
    <div class="note-item">
      <strong>Locked to NdPr basket pricing</strong>
      Magnet plants buy NdPr oxide as a single SKU at the ~25/75 natural separation ratio. Pure Pr<sub>6</sub>O<sub>11</sub> requires an additional separation cycle and trades at a modest premium when needed for ceramic pigments or didymium glass.
    </div>
    <div class="note-item">
      <strong>Magnet demand wildcard</strong>
      Pr demand tracks total NdFeB magnet output (EVs, wind turbines, consumer electronics). Forecasts of EV traction motor magnet demand drive Pr/Nd basket pricing more than any other factor.
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
