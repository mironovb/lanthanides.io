<div class="element-section info-block">
  <h2 class="section-title">Important Notes</h2>
  <div class="notes-block">
    <div class="note-item note-warning">
      <strong>Export controls in force</strong>
      Yttrium is one of the seven medium and heavy rare earths controlled under MOFCOM/GAC Announcement No.&thinsp;18/2025 [2]. Reported United States yttrium imports fell more than 90% in the first eight months after these controls took effect.
    </div>
    <div class="note-item">
      <strong>Largest retail-to-bulk spread</strong>
      Yttrium oxide bulk benchmarks sit near $7&ndash;$10/kg, while gram-scale metal samples on eBay/Alibaba exceed $9,000/kg. The spread reflects the cost of metallisation, hand-portioning, and Western retail margin &mdash; not material scarcity.
    </div>
    <div class="note-item">
      <strong>YBCO superconductor demand wildcard</strong>
      Fusion energy programmes (Commonwealth Fusion, Tokamak Energy) and HTS power cable rollouts could materially shift Y demand from the bulk-oxide ceramic market into a high-purity, high-margin segment over 2026&ndash;2030.
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
