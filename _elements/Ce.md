<div class="element-section info-block">
  <h2 class="section-title">Important Notes</h2>
  <div class="notes-block">
    <div class="note-item">
      <strong>Not on the MOFCOM control list</strong>
      Cerium is excluded from the No.&thinsp;18/2025 medium/heavy REE control basket. Bulk CeO<sub>2</sub> trades freely, though mixed-RE concentrate shipments may encounter classification scrutiny.
    </div>
    <div class="note-item">
      <strong>Co-production overhang</strong>
      Ce is produced in massive surplus relative to its end-use demand because Nd/Pr economics drive the basket. The result is chronic Ce oversupply and very low oxide pricing &mdash; the inverse of the heavy-RE supply tightness.
    </div>
    <div class="note-item">
      <strong>Display polishing dependency</strong>
      Every smartphone, tablet, monitor, and TV cover glass passes through CeO<sub>2</sub> polishing. Substitution by colloidal silica is possible for some applications but adds CMP cycle time and cost.
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
